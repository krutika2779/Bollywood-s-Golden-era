Top 10 Movies belongs to Golden era



    1. Mughal-E-Azam (1960)

Actors:- Prithviraj Kapoor as Emperor Akbar.

Madhubala as Anarkali.

Durga Khote as Maharani Jodha Bai.

Directed by:- K.Asif





    2. Chalti Ka Naam Gaadi (1958)

Actors:- Ashok Kumar as Brijmohan Sharma.

Madhubala as Renu.

Anoop Kumar as Jagmohan "Jagu" Sharma.

Kishore Kumar as Manmohan "Manu" Sharma

Directed by:- Satyen Bose.





    3. Mother India (1957)

Actors:- Nargis Dutt as Radha Singh.

Raaj kumar as Radha's husband.

Sunil dutt, Kanahiyalal, Jilloo maa, kumkum.

Directed by:- Mehboob Khan.





    4. Pyaasa (1957)

Actors:- Guru Dutt as Vijay.

Mala Sinha as Meena Ghosh.

Waheeda Rehman as Gulabo - This was her first major leading role in Hindi cinema.

Rehman as Mr.Ghosh.

Johnny Walker as Abdul Sattar.

Leela Mishra as Vijay's mother.

Kumkum as Juhi.

Directed by:- Guru Dutt.





    5. Do Aankhen Barah Haath (1957)

Actors:- V. Shantaram as Adinath.

Sandhya as Champa.

Baburao Pendharkar as Superintendent.

Ulhas as Shanker Passi.

B. M. Vyas as Jalia Nai.

Directed by:- V. Shantaram.





    6. Naya Daur (1957)

Actors:- Dilip Kumar as Shankar.

Vyjayanthimala as Rajni.

Ajit as Krishna.

Jeevan as Kundan.

Johnny Walker as Anjana.

Directed by:- B. R. Chopra.





    7. Shree 420 (1955)

Actors:- Nargis as Vidya.

Nadira as Maya.

Raj Kapoor as Ranbir Raj.

Directed by:- Raj Kapoor.





    8. Awaara (1951)

Actors:- Prithviraj Kapoor as Judge Raghunath.

Raj Kapoor as Raj.

Nargis as Rita.

K. N. Singh as Jagga.

Directed by:- Raj Kapoor.





    9. Mahal (1949)

Actors:- Ashok Kumar as Hari Shankar.

Madhubala as Kamini.

Directed by:- Kamal Amrohi.





    10. Andaz (1949)

Actors:- Nargis as Neena.

Dilip Kumar as Dilip.

Raj Kapoor as Rajan.

Directed by:- Mehboob Khan.
